## Tips for Screenshots

- **Resolution:** As high as possible (Retina if available)
- **Browser:** Safari or Firefox
- **Window:** Full width, but not necessarily full height
- **Data:** Record a few example actions so the view isn't empty
- **Save location:** `/Anleitung/images/`

## Tips for Videos

- **Format:** MP4 recommended
- **Length:** Short and concise (10-30 seconds)
- **Audio:** Not needed (embedded without sound)
- **Upload:** Google Drive and insert link in the app
